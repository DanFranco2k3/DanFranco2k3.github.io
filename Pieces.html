Pieces

Scroll down to know all of the 16 pieces clashing
across the board.

the Pawn moves one square straight ahead; can also move 2 squares from where it seats.


the Rook can move in its line or row.


the Knight jumps in making the shortest move that is not a straight one, an L-shape.


the Bishop moves diagonally.


the Queen may move like a Rook or a Bishop.


The King moves from its square to a neighboring square.
